Storage Upgrade HP 245 G8

User complains that 256GB storage isn't enough but doesn't want to change the original NVME drive since it's way over the budget. Unfortunately it doesn't come with SATA cable or bracket. As a workaround, I've installed a new 256GB SATA drive using bracket and cable from HP 14s since both are compatible.
